How to monetize your site

Written By

Sergio Nicolini

Hi everyone, today we are going to see how to monetize your website.

Google Adsense

Google Adsense is a very well-known site where you can monetize your site through ads, but it's focused on high-medium traffic sites.

Pros

• Google support, ads type

Cons

• Low CPM

Total

• 8/10

Adsterra

Adsterra is a less known platform where you can monetize your site through ads, and it's focused on low traffic sites.

Pros

• High CPM, good support, instant approval

Cons

• Intrusive ads

Total

• 8.5/10
